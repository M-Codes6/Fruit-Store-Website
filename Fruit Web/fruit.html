Fresh Fruits

There are many variations of fruits available in the store.

Best Fresh Grapes

Juicy and sweet, grapes are not only delicious but also packed with antioxidants, promoting heart health and overall well-being.

Best Fresh Guava

Bursting with tropical flavor, guava is a nutrient-rich fruit known for its high vitamin C content, aiding in immune support and digestion.

Best Fresh Pineapple

A tropical delight, pineapple is both refreshing and rich in enzymes, promoting digestion and offering a sweet and tangy taste experience.

Best Fresh Sitafal

"Sitafal" refers to the Custard Apple, which is a tropical fruit known for its sweet and custard-like flavor. The scientific name for Custard Apple is Annona squamosa. The fruit is greenish-brown on the outside with soft, creamy white flesh on the inside. It contains black seeds and is enjoyed for its unique taste.

Best Fresh Watermelon

Watermelon is a refreshing and hydrating fruit known for its vibrant red or pink flesh, embedded with black seeds or seedless varieties. With its high water content and sweet taste, watermelon is a perfect summer treat, offering a delightful blend of juiciness and natural sweetness.
